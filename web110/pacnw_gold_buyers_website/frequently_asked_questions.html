How will I receive my cash deposit in just 24 hours?

The staff at Pacific Northwest Gold Buyers work 24 hours a day, seven days a week. As soon as we receive your precious metals for processing via FedX or USPS, we immediately process all of your material. This process usually takes less then 1 hour. As soon as we finish processing your order we send you an email with the exact dollar amount according to your submission. At this point you can ether accept or reject our offer. Once you accept our offer we immediately deposit the funds into your account via PayPal or BECU. These funds are pre approved by our banking institution and your funds are immediately available to you.

How do I know if my jewelry is 14k or 18k gold?

The best way to figure out the purity of your gold or Platinum jewelry is to look for whats called a ”Makers Mark” or a "Hallmark" located somewhere on the inside of your rings, or on the clasp of your necklaces. These hallmarks can be very small and sometimes impossible to see without the proper equipment. If your lucky enough you will be able to see the numbers 10k, 14, 18k, 22k, or other hallmarks that indicate your jewelry is real gold, silver or platinum. You can refer to our list of hallmarks for more information on how to find out if your jewelry is real.

Do you buy damaged necklace's and broken rings?

Yes, we buy all broken, damaged, smashed, twisted, melted, or otherwise unwanted yet still valuable jewelry. We also buy scrap gold and silver, meaning you can send us small pieces of gold necklaces or bracelets. We accept any and all forms of precious metals.

The diamonds in my ring are very small, will I still get paid for them?

Yes, unlike most gold buyers, we pay you for your diamonds no matter what size they are. As long as the diamonds in your jewelry are real you get paid no matter how big or small they are. Believe it or not small diamonds can be worth a lot of money.
